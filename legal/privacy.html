Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2024

Kwanzaa Pocket Guide ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect and how it is used.

Information We Collect

Automatically Generated Data

  • Device Identity: When you first use the Community Board, the app generates a cryptographic keypair (public/private key) stored locally on your device. This keypair is used to sign your posts and verify your identity. The private key never leaves your device.
  • Display Name: You may optionally set a display name. If not set, a random identifier is generated from your public key.

User-Generated Content

  • Community Posts: Messages you post to the Community Board are shared publicly with other users via a peer-to-peer (P2P) network.
  • Journal Entries: Personal journal entries are stored locally on your device only and are never transmitted.

Information We Do NOT Collect

  • Real name or contact information
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers (IDFA, Android ID)
  • Usage analytics or tracking data
  • Payment information

How Your Data is Shared

The Community Board uses Gun.js, a decentralized peer-to-peer database. When you post to the Community Board:

  • Your post, display name, and public key are shared with all users of the app
  • Data syncs directly between devices and through relay servers
  • Posts are public and cannot be made private
  • We do not control or have access to the P2P network data

Data Storage

  • Local Storage: Your keypair, display name, journal entries, blocked users, and hidden posts are stored locally on your device.
  • P2P Network: Community posts are distributed across the decentralized Gun.js network and may persist indefinitely on other users' devices.

Data Deletion

  • Local Data: Uninstall the app or clear app data to remove all locally stored information.
  • Community Posts: Due to the decentralized nature of the P2P network, posts cannot be guaranteed to be deleted from all devices. However, you can delete posts from your local view.

Children's Privacy

This app is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. The Community Board feature is rated for users 12+ due to user-generated content.

Security

Your private signing key is stored securely on your device and is never transmitted. All posts are cryptographically signed to prevent impersonation.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy in the app.

Contact Us
